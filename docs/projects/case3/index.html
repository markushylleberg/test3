BUILDING BEGINS OCTOBER 2018

The city of Gothenborg was our first customer after we launched the Copenhagen project. Gothenborg University and Gothenborg Commune is finansing this project and the goal is to make 72 Rigs, which equels to 864 student apartments. We're hoping to launch these Urban Riggers by the end of 2022. Gothenborg is one of Swedens main study cities along with Stockholm and Malmö. Gothenborg Commune discovered Urban Rigger trough our Copenhagen project, and saw it as an sustainable and amazing way to help solve the lack of student apartments.
